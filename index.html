Fair In-Employment Practices

üí∞ Fair Remuneration

Base pay on:

  • Job value
  • Individual contribution
  • Skills & experience
  • Market benchmarks

NOT on:

  • Previous salary
  • Protected characteristics
  • Personal relationships
  • Non-job factors

üìä Performance Management

  • Clear, measurable KPIs
  • Regular reviews (quarterly recommended)
  • Two-way feedback valued
  • Documentation of all reviews
  • Appeal process available

üìö Training & Development

  • Equal access to opportunities
  • Selection based on potential
  • Support for skills upgrading
  • No discrimination in programs

üìà Promotion & Posting

  • Open communication of opportunities
  • Objective selection criteria
  • Consistent evaluation process
  • Feedback for unsuccessful candidates
